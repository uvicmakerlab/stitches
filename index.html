The Frames of Remaking

"Frame" as in "Useful" (framian)
Gateways and filters for knowledge production
Negotiated and enacted, not possessed
Prompted by attention to transduction
N-dimensional, or "indefinite number of perspectives" (McGann 15)
Entanglements of materialism + idealism, human + nonhuman

Image care of Jentery Sayers
For more on entanglements and "agential realism," see Karen Barad (2007)
For more on texts in n-dimensions, see Jerome McGann (2003)
